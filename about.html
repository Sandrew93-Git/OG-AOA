Jesse

We have each come from different fishing backgrounds. Jesse grew up in the Durham region, Fishing many streams and tributaries feeding into Lake Ontario. Focusing much of his time on native trout, steelhead and salmon during their respective spawning seasons.


Andrew

Andrew grew up mostly on the shores of Lake Simcoe, with some camping and cottage trips in the northern muskoka areas. Cutting his teeth on ice fishing for perch, and wading into the lake fishing submerged structure for whatever swam by.


Mike

Mike's previous fishing experience was dominated by open water great lake fishing on boats, frequenting lake Erie.


The common thread that brought the group together was Jesse. Responsible for stoking Andrew's love of fishing into a roaring fire as they grew up as friends. And rekindling Mike's desire for fishing once they started working in the same field.
